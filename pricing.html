Frequently Asked Questions

How does the subscription work?
Once you subscribe, you'll get immediate access to all teacher resumes and can contact them directly. Your subscription renews automatically each month/year until you cancel.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes! You can cancel your subscription at any time from your dashboard. You'll continue to have access until the end of your current billing period.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express) through our secure payment processor Stripe. We'll be adding PayPal and other payment methods soon.
Is there a free trial?
Currently, we offer a free plan that lets you browse jobs and post your resume. To access teacher resumes and contact candidates, you'll need a Professional or Annual subscription.
Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?
Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time from your dashboard. Changes take effect immediately, and we'll prorate the charges accordingly.
